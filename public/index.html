Our Programs

Elementary (K–5)

Interactive learning through stories, games, and exploration. We foster curiosity and foundational skills in a joyful, supportive environment. Our curriculum encourages creativity and social development from the very start.

Middle School (6–8)

Project-based learning with real-world problem solving. We emphasize collaboration, critical thinking, and STEM education. Students engage with hands-on activities that build confidence and independence.

High School (9–12)

College & career readiness, leadership training, and innovation labs prepare students for a successful future. We offer advanced placement courses, internships, and mentoring to empower ambition and skill development.

About Monaco Education

Founded in 2020, Monaco Education is an innovative K–12 learning platform combining traditional academics with 21st-century skills. We focus on personal growth, creativity, and confidence.

Our certified educators use a tech-first approach and a supportive environment to help every learner rise to their potential with custom learning paths and enrichment options.

We collaborate with schools, parents, and communities to ensure every student’s needs are met with tailored resources, accessibility tools, and emotional support.

Monaco Education is committed to diversity, equity, and inclusion, fostering a culture where every student feels valued and empowered.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Monaco Education fully online?

Yes, we offer 100% online learning with optional in-person meetups in select locations for enrichment and social engagement.

Are your teachers certified?

All educators are state-certified and experienced in online and hybrid learning environments.

What curriculum do you use?

We follow U.S. Common Core standards and integrate STEM, arts, and social-emotional learning modules for a holistic approach.

Do you support special education?

Absolutely. We provide IEP support, accessibility accommodations, and trained staff for diverse learning needs.

Can students access recorded lessons?

Yes, all lessons are recorded and available for review anytime through our student portal.
